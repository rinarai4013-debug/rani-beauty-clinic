# SOP: New Peptide Patient Onboarding
**Target Timeline:** 7-14 days | **Revenue:** $299-399/mo

---

## Step 1: Receive Intake (Day 0)
- Parse intake form
- Determine peptide: NAD+, Sermorelin, Glutathione, GHK-Cu, PT-141, BPC-157
- Check medical history for kidney/liver disease or diabetes (labs required if present)
- Create patient file
- Add to pipeline: PIPELINE-NEW

## Step 2: Send Welcome (Day 0)
- Welcome text + email customized for peptide type
- Apply Mangomint tags: PEPTIDE-PATIENT, specific peptide tag

## Step 3: Labs (If Required)
- Only needed for patients with kidney/liver disease or diabetes
- CMP + eGFR at minimum
- Most peptide patients: skip to Step 4

## Step 4: Qualiphy GFE (Day 1-5)
- Send Qualiphy evaluation link
- Move to GFE-PENDING
- Follow up at Day 2 and Day 4

## Step 5: MD Review & Rx (Day 3-9)
- Medical Director reviews via Qualiphy
- Approval -> ship medication
- Move to MED-SHIPPED

## Step 6: First Dose + Ongoing
- Provide administration instructions specific to peptide type
- Move to ACTIVE-PATIENT
- Monthly check-ins
- Cross-sell NAD+ IV drip add-on
