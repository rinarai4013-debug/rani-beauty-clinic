# SOP: Quarterly Labs (90-Day Re-Check)

---

## Who Needs Quarterly Labs
- ALL GLP-1 patients (required for compliance)
- ALL hormone therapy patients
- Peptide patients with kidney/liver disease or diabetes

## Required Panels
- GLP-1: CMP, Lipid Panel, HbA1c, TSH + Free T4
- Hormones: Same as initial panel (Total/Free Testosterone, Estradiol, DHEA-S, Cortisol, TSH/FT4, CMP)

## Process
1. At Month 3: Flag patient for quarterly labs
2. Send lab reminder text
3. Apply Mangomint tag: LABS-DUE
4. Follow lab reminder cadence (Day 1/3/5)
5. Once received: MD reviews via Qualiphy
6. Adjust dose/treatment if needed
7. Update patient file with lab results date
8. Remove LABS-DUE tag, set next lab date (+90 days)
