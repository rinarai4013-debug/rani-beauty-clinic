# SOP: Churn Save (Patient Wants to Cancel)

---

## Common Reasons & Responses

### "It's too expensive"
- Offer dose adjustment (lower dose = lower price)
- Highlight value: $299-499/mo vs. $1,300+/mo at brand-name clinics
- Mention VIP Transform package for better value
- Never discount. Reframe value instead.

### "It's not working"
- Ask about timeline expectations (results typically visible Month 2-3)
- Check if they're on optimal dose (may need titration up)
- Ask about diet/lifestyle factors
- Offer MD consultation for dosing review

### "Side effects are too bad"
- Validate their experience
- Ask specifics (nausea, fatigue, constipation)
- Suggest: smaller meals, hydration, ginger for nausea
- Offer dose reduction
- Flag for MD review

### "I reached my goal"
- Celebrate!
- Discuss maintenance options (lower dose maintenance)
- Transition to peptides or wellness injections
- Ask for referral + Google review
