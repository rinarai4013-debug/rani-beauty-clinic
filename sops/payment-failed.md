# SOP: Payment Failed / Card Declined

---

## Process
1. Identify failed payment (Mangomint notification or manual check)
2. Send friendly text immediately (Day 0)
3. Follow up Day 2 if not resolved
4. Day 5: Flag AT-RISK if still unresolved
5. Do NOT ship medication until payment resolves

## Day 0 - Payment Failed (SMS)

Hi [FIRST_NAME]! Quick heads up, it looks like the card on file didn't go through for your [MEDICATION] refill. No worries! Can you give us a call at (425) 539-4440 or reply with an updated card so we can get your refill processed? I don't want there to be any gap in your treatment!

## Day 2 - Follow Up (SMS)

Hey [FIRST_NAME]! Just checking in on the payment update. I want to make sure we get your [MEDICATION] shipped ASAP so you don't miss a dose. You can call us at (425) 539-4440 to update your card, or reply here and I can help. Takes just a minute!

## Day 5 - Escalation (SMS)

Hi [FIRST_NAME], I haven't been able to reach you about the payment update. I know things come up! I just want to make sure you don't lose momentum on your progress. Can we connect this week? I'm available anytime at (425) 539-4440

---
**Note:** Always be empathetic. Never make the patient feel embarrassed about a declined card.
