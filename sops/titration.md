## When to Titrate Up
- Patient tolerating current dose well (minimal side effects)
- Weight loss has plateaued at current dose
- At least 4 weeks on current dose
- MD approval via Qualiphy

## When to Hold or Lower
- Significant nausea/vomiting lasting >5 days
- Patient request
- MD direction based on labs or symptoms

## Process
1. Monthly check-in: assess tolerance and progress
2. If titration appropriate: flag for MD review
3. MD approves new dose via Qualiphy
4. New dose shipped
5. Update Mangomint dose tag (SEMA-D1 -> SEMA-D2, etc.)
6. Update patient file and pipeline
7. Send titration instructions to patient
8. Update MRR (higher dose = higher price)
