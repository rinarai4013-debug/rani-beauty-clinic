# SOP: Emergency / Severe Side Effects Response

---

## IMMEDIATE ACTION REQUIRED FOR:
- Severe abdominal pain (possible pancreatitis)
- Allergic reaction (swelling, difficulty breathing, rash)
- Suicidal ideation or severe mood changes
- Signs of thyroid tumor (lump in neck, difficulty swallowing, hoarseness)
- Severe dehydration (unable to keep fluids down for 24+ hours)

## Steps
1. **INSTRUCT PATIENT TO CALL 911 OR GO TO ER IMMEDIATELY**
2. **NOTIFY RINA IMMEDIATELY** (call, not text)
3. Document the interaction in patient file with exact timestamp
4. Do NOT advise on medical treatment (that is the MD/ER's role)
5. Follow up with patient within 24 hours after ER visit
6. Flag for MD review before resuming medication
7. Update patient file with full incident details

## Emergency Response Text (SMS)

[FIRST_NAME], I'm so sorry you're experiencing this. Please go to your nearest emergency room right away or call 911. Your safety comes first. I'm notifying our medical team right now. Please text or call me at (425) 539-4440 once you've been seen so I can follow up.

## Post-Emergency Follow-Up (SMS - send 24 hours after)

Hi [FIRST_NAME], I've been thinking about you. How are you feeling today? I hope the ER visit went well. Our Medical Director will review everything before we make any changes to your treatment plan. Please rest and take care of yourself. I'm here whenever you need me. (425) 539-4440

---

**COMPLIANCE:** Rina does NOT diagnose, prescribe, or advise on emergency medical treatment. Always direct to ER/911. Always notify MD.
