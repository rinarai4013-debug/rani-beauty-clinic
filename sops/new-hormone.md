# SOP: New Hormone Therapy Patient Onboarding
**Target Timeline:** 10-14 days | **Revenue:** $249-499/mo

---

## Step 1: Receive Intake (Day 0)
- Parse intake form
- Determine hormone type: Testosterone, Thyroid, DHEA
- Create patient file
- Add to pipeline: PIPELINE-NEW

## Step 2: Send Welcome (Day 0)
- Welcome text + email customized for hormone therapy
- Apply Mangomint tags: HORMONE-PATIENT

## Step 3: Labs (ALWAYS Required) (Day 0-5)
- Required panels: Total/Free Testosterone, Estradiol, DHEA-S, Cortisol, TSH/FT4, CMP
- Send lab order
- Move to LABS-NEEDED
- Follow lab reminder cadence (Day 1/3/5)

## Step 4: Qualiphy GFE (Day 3-7)
- Send Qualiphy evaluation link
- Move to GFE-PENDING

## Step 5: MD Review & Rx (Day 5-9)
- Medical Director reviews labs + GFE
- Dosing determination based on lab values
- Approval -> ship medication

## Step 6: First Dose + Ongoing
- Administration instructions specific to hormone type
- Move to ACTIVE-PATIENT
- Monthly check-ins
- Quarterly labs required
- Cross-sell: B12 + Vitamin D combo

---
**COMPLIANCE:** Rina does NOT diagnose or prescribe. All medical decisions = Medical Director via Qualiphy. Quarterly labs required for ongoing hormone patients.
