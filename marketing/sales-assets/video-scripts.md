## Production Notes

**Equipment:** iPhone 15 Pro or higher (4K, natural light preferred), wireless lav mic
**Editing:** Clean cuts, subtle text overlays, brand colors (Navy/Gold/Cream)
**Music:** Ambient, sophisticated (royalty-free)
**Captions:** Always include — 85% of social video is watched without sound
**CTA overlay:** "Book Your Reveal Assessment" + ranibeautyclinic.com/the-reveal
**Consent:** Patient release forms required for any patient appearing on camera
**Brand compliance:** Never use "infusion" (IM injections only), never mention internal revenue goals, always say "physician-supervised"
