### 1. Membership Overview

The Inner Circle is an exclusive membership program offered by Rani Beauty Clinic ("Clinic") for patients enrolled in or who have completed The Reveal skin restoration protocol. Membership provides ongoing benefits designed to support long-term skin health and maintenance following your Reveal protocol.

### 2. Membership Benefits

As an Inner Circle member, you receive:

- **Priority Scheduling** — Access to preferred appointment slots, including early morning and evening availability, before they are released to the general booking system.
- **10% Discount on Maintenance Treatments** — Applies to Sofwave, Secret RF, HydraFacial, and chemical peel treatments booked during your active membership. Excludes injectable treatments, GLP-1 programs, and laser hair removal packages.
- **Quarterly Skin Assessments** — Complimentary skin evaluation with progress photography every 3 months during your membership.
- **Direct Provider Messaging** — Ability to message your treatment provider for non-urgent skin questions via our secure messaging system.
- **Exclusive Access** — Early access to new protocols, technologies, and treatment offerings before public release.
- **Members-Only Events** — Invitations to educational workshops, product launches, and member appreciation events hosted by Rani Beauty Clinic.

### 3. Membership Terms

- **Monthly Fee:** $99/month (or included at no additional cost with the Complete Reveal Tier for the first 3 months)
- **Billing:** Charged to the payment method on file on the enrollment date of each month
- **Minimum Term:** No minimum commitment. Month-to-month enrollment.
- **Cancellation:** You may cancel at any time by providing written notice (email to info@ranibeautyclinic.com or in-person at the clinic) at least 15 days before your next billing date. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period.
- **Pause:** You may pause your membership for up to 2 consecutive months per calendar year by contacting us at least 15 days before your next billing date. Benefits are suspended during the pause period.

### 4. Discount Terms

- The 10% maintenance treatment discount applies to the listed retail price at the time of service
- Discount cannot be combined with other promotions, packages, or membership discounts (including Angel Glow Up membership pricing)
- Discount applies to the member only and is non-transferable
- Discount does not apply to products, retail skincare, or treatments not listed in Section 2

### 5. General Terms

- Membership is personal and non-transferable
- Rani Beauty Clinic reserves the right to modify membership benefits with 30 days written notice to members
- Unused quarterly assessments do not roll over between quarters
- All treatments are subject to clinical assessment and provider approval; membership does not guarantee suitability for any specific treatment
- All treatments at Rani Beauty Clinic are performed under physician supervision
