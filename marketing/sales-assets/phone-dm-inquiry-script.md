# Phone & DM Inquiry Script
## For handling inbound inquiries about The Reveal and post-GLP-1 skin tightening

---

## Phone Script

### Greeting
"Thank you for calling Rani Beauty Clinic, this is {name}. How can I help you today?"

### If asking about skin tightening / loose skin / The Reveal:

**Step 1: Acknowledge and validate**
"Absolutely, we work with a lot of patients who are dealing with skin changes after weight loss. You're definitely not alone in this. Can I ask a couple of quick questions so I can point you in the right direction?"

**Step 2: Qualify**
- "Are you currently on a GLP-1 medication like Ozempic, Wegovy, or Mounjaro, or have you been in the past?"
- "Approximately how much weight have you lost?"
- "What areas are you most concerned about — face, neck, body, or a combination?"

**Step 3: Educate briefly**
"Based on what you're describing, you'd be a great candidate for a Reveal Assessment. The Reveal is our signature protocol that combines two FDA-cleared technologies — Sofwave ultrasound and RF microneedling — specifically designed for post-weight-loss skin restoration. We treat the deep layer and the surface layer together, which is what makes it more effective than a single treatment."

**Step 4: Book**
"The first step is a complimentary Reveal Assessment — it takes about 30 minutes, and our clinical team will evaluate your skin, discuss your goals, and map out a personalized plan. There's no pressure and no obligation. Can I get you scheduled?"

**Step 5: Capture info**
- First name, last name
- Phone number and email
- Preferred day/time
- GLP-1 medication and approximate timeline

"Perfect, I have you booked for {date/time}. You'll receive a confirmation text. When you arrive, just check in at the front desk and let them know you're here for a Reveal Assessment. Do you have any questions before then?"

### If asking about price specifically:
"Great question. The Reveal comes in three tiers depending on the level of treatment you need — they start at $1,490 and go up to $5,990 for the most comprehensive protocol. We also accept HSA/FSA and offer financing through Cherry and PatientFi. The best way to know which tier fits your needs is through the complimentary assessment — would you like to schedule one?"

### If hesitant:
"I completely understand. If it helps, the assessment is totally free and there's no obligation. It's really just a chance for you to understand your options and see what makes sense for your skin. Most patients tell us the assessment was the most helpful part of the whole process. What do you think — would you like to come in and at least take a look?"

## Key Phrases to Use
- "Specifically designed for post-weight-loss skin"
- "Dual-technology approach" (not "two machines")
- "Complimentary assessment" (not "free consultation")
- "No pressure, no obligation"
- "Your skin transformation"
- "Physician-supervised"

## Key Phrases to Avoid
- "Loose skin" (use "skin changes" or "laxity" until the patient uses the word first)
- "Anti-aging" (focus on restoration, not fighting age)
- "Infusion" (NEVER — Rani does IM injections only)
- "Cheap" or "affordable" (use "accessible" or "manageable monthly payments")
- "Deal" or "discount" (use "investment" or "protocol")
