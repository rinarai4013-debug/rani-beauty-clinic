# The Reveal — Consultation Script
## Complementary module for RaniOS Prompt 05 (Consult Script)

This script extends the existing consultation framework for patients specifically inquiring about post-GLP-1 skin restoration or The Reveal protocol.

---

## Pre-Consult Prep (Provider Notes)

Before the patient arrives, review:
- GLP-1 medication type and duration (from intake form)
- Approximate weight loss amount
- Primary areas of concern (from intake or booking notes)
- Whether they downloaded the Skin Guide or came through The Reveal page
- Any previous aesthetic treatments

---

## Phase 1: Connection & Discovery (5-7 minutes)

### Opening (warm, validating)

"Hi {name}, welcome to Rani Beauty Clinic. I'm {provider_name}. Before we dive into anything clinical, I just want to say — congratulations on your transformation. That takes real commitment, and it's worth acknowledging."

*Pause. Let them respond. Many patients light up here — they rarely hear this from providers.*

"Tell me a little about your journey. When did you start your medication, and how has the experience been for you?"

### Discovery Questions

Listen for emotional cues. GLP-1 skin patients often feel frustrated that their body doesn't match their effort.

1. "What changes in your skin are bothering you most right now?"
2. "When did you first start noticing these changes — was it early in your weight loss or more recently?"
3. "Is there a specific area that affects your confidence the most?" *(face vs. body — this drives protocol recommendation)*
4. "Have you looked into any treatments before coming in today? What did you find?"
5. "What would it mean for you to feel like your skin matched the rest of your transformation?"

*The last question is key. It shifts from clinical problem to emotional outcome. Remember the answer — you'll reference it when presenting the protocol.*

---

## Phase 2: Education & Assessment (8-10 minutes)

### The Explanation (simple, non-technical)

"So here's what's happening biologically — and it's completely normal. When you lose weight quickly with a GLP-1 medication, the fat decreases faster than your skin's collagen can adapt. Think of collagen as the scaffolding that holds your skin up. When the volume underneath decreases rapidly, that scaffolding gets stretched and can't bounce back on its own.

This isn't a skincare problem — no cream or supplement is going to rebuild that structural collagen. It requires technology that actually stimulates your body to produce new collagen at a deep level."

### The Physical Assessment

"Let me take a closer look at your areas of concern. Can you show me what's bothering you most?"

*Assess and verbalize what you see:*
- "I can see some laxity along the jawline here — this is very common with facial volume loss."
- "The neck area shows some textural changes — this is the superficial layer responding to the volume change underneath."
- "Your skin quality is actually quite good in terms of elasticity — which means you'll likely respond very well to treatment."

*Always include at least one positive observation. Patients need to hear that their skin has recovery potential.*

---

## Phase 3: Protocol Presentation (5-7 minutes)

### Introduce The Reveal

"Based on what I'm seeing, I want to tell you about something we developed specifically for patients in your situation. It's called The Reveal.

The Reveal combines two technologies — Sofwave ultrasound and Secret RF microneedling — because post-weight-loss skin is a dual-layer problem. Sofwave goes deep — 1.5 millimeters beneath the surface — to rebuild the collagen foundation. Secret RF targets the surface layer for texture, pores, and superficial tightening. Together, they produce results that neither technology achieves alone."

### Present Tiers (always present from top down)

"There are three tiers, and I'll walk through each so you can see where you might fit:

**The Complete** is our most comprehensive protocol — one Sofwave session plus four Secret RF sessions covering face and body zones, with progress tracking, a full skincare system, and three months of Inner Circle membership. This is $5,990 and it's designed for patients who want maximum restoration across multiple areas.

**The Signature** is actually our most popular tier. It includes one Sofwave session and two Secret RF sessions focused on the face and neck, plus skincare and 90-day progress tracking. This is $3,990.

**The Essential** is two Secret RF sessions for a single zone — great for targeted improvement in one area. That's $1,490.

Based on what I'm seeing with your skin, I'd recommend the **{recommended_tier}** because {personalized_reason}."

*Always tie the recommendation back to their specific assessment findings and their emotional goal from Phase 1.*

---

## Phase 4: Investment Discussion (3-5 minutes)

### Framing (value, not discount)

"The investment for the {recommended_tier} is ${price}. That includes everything we discussed — the treatments, the skincare, the progress tracking. When you think about the value compared to surgical alternatives, which can range from $15,000 to $40,000 with significant downtime and recovery, The Reveal delivers comprehensive restoration at a fraction of that cost and zero surgery."

### Financing (present proactively, not reactively)

"I should mention — most of our Reveal patients use one of our financing options. We work with Cherry and PatientFi, and both let you check your rate without affecting your credit. Many patients end up at around ${monthly_estimate} per month, which makes this very accessible. We also accept HSA and FSA cards."

---

## Phase 5: Objection Handling

### "I need to think about it."
"Of course — this is a meaningful investment and you should feel confident in your decision. What I'd encourage you to think about is timing. Collagen remodeling takes 3-6 months to reach full results. The sooner you start, the sooner you'll see your transformation complete. Would it help to lock in your Assessment photos today so we have a baseline ready when you decide?"

### "That's more than I expected."
"I understand. Let me show you what financing looks like — many patients are surprised at how manageable the monthly payment is. Also, remember that this is a comprehensive protocol, not a single treatment. The value of combining both technologies in a structured sequence is what drives the results."

### "Can I just do one treatment instead of the full protocol?"
"Absolutely — the Essential tier is a great starting point, and you can always add sessions later. What I want to be transparent about is that post-weight-loss skin typically needs both technologies to address the full picture. A single treatment will improve one layer, but you may find that you want the comprehensive approach for the best outcome."

### "My friend got X treatment and it worked."
"That's great to hear. Every patient responds differently, and what works for one person's skin type and degree of laxity may differ from another's. What makes The Reveal unique is that it's specifically designed for post-weight-loss skin — we've mapped the protocol to address the exact type of collagen loss that happens with GLP-1 medications. If you'd like, we can evaluate how {friend's treatment} fits into your plan."

### "I want to wait until I reach my goal weight."
"That's a common instinct, and I understand the logic. But here's what the science shows — collagen takes months to rebuild. If you start now, your skin is already producing new collagen while you're still losing weight. By the time you reach your goal, your skin has had a significant head start. Waiting until the end means you're starting from a bigger deficit."

### If booking today:
"Wonderful. Let me get your first session scheduled. We'll take your baseline photos today so we can track your progress from day one. You'll also receive a pre-treatment guide with everything you need to prepare. Do you have any final questions before we lock in your date?"

### If not booking today:
"No problem at all. Here's what I'd suggest — take the guide home, talk it over, and when you're ready, call us at (425) 539-4440 or book online. Your assessment findings are in our system, so when you come back, we can pick up right where we left off. Is there anything else I can answer for you today?"

## Integration with RaniOS Prompt 05

When the consult is for a Reveal Assessment, the existing Prompt 05 should detect the following signals and route to this script:
- Service booked includes "Reveal" or "skin tightening" or "post-weight-loss"
- Client intake mentions GLP-1 medications, Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound
- Client intake mentions weight loss, loose skin, skin laxity
- AI summary flags post-GLP-1 skin concerns

The Prompt 05 JSON output should include an additional field:
```json
"reveal_protocol_flag": true,
"reveal_recommended_tier": "Signature",
"reveal_primary_concern": "facial laxity and neck"
```
