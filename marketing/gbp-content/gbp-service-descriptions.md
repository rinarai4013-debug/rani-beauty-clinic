## Part 2: GBP Q&A Entries (15)

### Q1: What is The Reveal protocol?
The Reveal is our signature skin restoration protocol designed for patients who have experienced skin laxity after GLP-1 weight loss (Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound). It combines Sofwave ultrasound and Secret RF microneedling to address loose skin at two depths. Available in three tiers starting at $1,490 with complimentary assessments.

### Q2: Do you treat patients who are still on Ozempic/Wegovy?
Yes. You do not need to stop your GLP-1 medication to receive skin tightening treatments. Many patients begin The Reveal protocol while still actively losing weight. Starting early gives your collagen a head start on rebuilding.

### Q3: How much does Sofwave cost at Rani Beauty Clinic?
Sofwave pricing: Brow Lift $1,150, Lower Face $2,250, Neck $1,750, Full Face $2,250, Full Face + Neck $3,999. We also offer a Sofwave + RF Micro combo at $4,499. HSA/FSA accepted, financing available through Cherry and PatientFi.

### Q4: What is the difference between Sofwave and RF microneedling?
Sofwave uses focused ultrasound to stimulate collagen at 1.5mm depth (deep dermis) for lifting and tightening. RF microneedling (Secret RF) uses radiofrequency energy delivered through needles to remodel the superficial dermis for texture improvement. At Rani Beauty Clinic, we often combine both in our Reveal protocol for dual-layer restoration.

### Q5: Is there downtime after Sofwave?
No downtime with Sofwave. You can return to all normal activities, including applying makeup, immediately after treatment. Mild redness may occur for a few hours. Secret RF has 24-48 hours of mild redness similar to a sunburn.

### Q6: How long do skin tightening results last?
Sofwave results last 1-3 years depending on skin condition and lifestyle. The new collagen produced is permanent, though natural aging continues. Annual maintenance sessions help sustain results. Most patients see full results at 3-6 months after treatment.

### Q7: Do you offer financing for skin tightening treatments?
Yes. We accept Cherry and PatientFi financing with no credit impact for pre-qualification. HSA and FSA cards are also accepted. Our Reveal protocol tiers range from $1,490 to $5,990, and monthly payment plans are available.

### Q8: What causes loose skin after weight loss?
Rapid fat loss (common with GLP-1 medications) outpaces your skin's collagen network. The collagen and elastin fibers that support skin structure cannot contract fast enough to keep up with volume loss. This results in laxity, sagging, textural changes, and volume loss — particularly in the face, neck, arms, and abdomen.

### Q9: Can skin tightening help with "Ozempic face"?
Yes. "Ozempic face" refers to facial volume loss and laxity that occurs during GLP-1 weight loss. Sofwave is particularly effective for lifting and tightening the brow, jawline, and neck. For more comprehensive restoration, our Reveal protocol adds RF microneedling for texture and surface improvement.

### Q10: Is Sofwave safe for all skin types?
Yes. Sofwave is FDA-cleared and safe for all skin types and tones (Fitzpatrick I-VI). Unlike some laser treatments that carry higher risk for darker skin, ultrasound-based Sofwave works beneath the skin surface and does not interact with melanin.

### Q11: What's included in a Reveal Assessment?
The Reveal Assessment is a complimentary consultation that includes full facial and/or body skin evaluation, skin quality analysis, discussion of your GLP-1 journey and goals, personalized treatment mapping, protocol recommendation, and baseline photography. Takes approximately 30 minutes with no obligation.

### Q12: Do you accept HSA/FSA for aesthetic treatments?
Yes. Many of our treatments qualify for HSA/FSA payment. This includes Sofwave, RF microneedling, and Reveal protocol treatments. Check with your HSA/FSA administrator for specific coverage details.

### Q13: How is Rani Beauty Clinic different from other medspas?
Rani Beauty Clinic is physician-supervised with a board-certified Medical Director overseeing all treatments. We specialize in post-GLP-1 skin restoration with dual-technology protocols. Located in Renton, WA, we serve South King County with advanced technology (Sofwave, Cutera Secret RF, Candela GentleMax Pro Plus) and personalized treatment plans.

### Q14: Can I get skin tightening on my body (not just face)?
Yes. Secret RF microneedling is effective on body areas including abdomen, arms, thighs, and knees. Sofwave is also used for body tightening. Our Complete Reveal tier includes both face and body zone treatments for patients wanting comprehensive post-weight-loss restoration.

### Q15: What is the best non-surgical treatment for loose skin after weight loss?
For most patients, a combination approach works best. At Rani Beauty Clinic, our Reveal protocol combines Sofwave ultrasound (deep collagen stimulation) with Secret RF microneedling (surface remodeling) — addressing loose skin at two depths for results that single treatments cannot achieve. Complimentary assessments available.
