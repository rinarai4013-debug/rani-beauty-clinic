This calendar coordinates blog publishing, social media, GBP posts, email campaigns, and ad copy across a 12-week launch period. Blog posts are drawn from the 50 posts created in batches 4-7. Social content is generated by the existing `weekly-content-batch` scheduled task.

**Publishing cadence:**
- Blog: 4 posts/week (Mon, Wed, Thu, Sat)
- GBP: 1 post/week (Tuesday)
- Email nurture: Triggered by lead capture (5-email sequence)
- Social: Daily (generated by `weekly-content-batch`)

## Integration with Existing Systems

**`weekly-content-batch` (Sunday 6 AM):** This existing scheduled task generates social content (IG, Reels, Stories, GBP). The content calendar above provides the blog-specific schedule. Social content should reference and cross-promote blog posts published that week.

**`biweekly-ad-copy-refresh` (1st & 15th):** Meta ad creative refreshes should align with the calendar phases. Weeks 1-4 = awareness creative, Weeks 5-8 = education/specifics, Weeks 9-12 = local/conversion.

**`daily-review-monitor` (Daily 8 AM):** Monitor for reviews mentioning Reveal, skin tightening, or GLP-1 skin. Flag for priority response.

**Email nurture sequence:** Activated on lead capture (guide download or /the-reveal form). 5 emails over 12 days. Runs independently of the content calendar but coordinated in tone.
