## Campaign Management Notes

**Budget allocation:** 60% awareness (Ads 1-3), 40% retargeting (Ads 4-5)
**Pixel events:** Track: PageView, ViewContent (/the-reveal), Lead (form submit), Schedule (Mangomint booking click)
**Custom audiences to build:**
- Website visitors (30 days)
- /the-reveal page visitors (30 days)
- Lead magnet downloaders (60 days)
- Video viewers (75% watched, 30 days)
- Engaged IG/FB (90 days)
**Lookalikes:** 1% of existing clients (from Airtable export), 1% of Reveal inquiries
**Exclusions:** Existing clients with Reveal booking, current patients (suppress list from Airtable)
**Frequency cap:** 3x/week for awareness, 5x/week for retargeting
**A/B testing:** Rotate creative every 2 weeks; test headlines and primary text variations
**Integration:** Coordinate with `biweekly-ad-copy-refresh` scheduled task and RaniOS Prompt 14 (ad copy generation) for ongoing optimization
