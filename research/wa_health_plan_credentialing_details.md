# Washington State Health Plan Credentialing Details
## Source: Foundation for Health Care Quality - Credentialing Transition to CAQH (V7, Jan 6, 2025)
## URL: https://www.qualityhealth.org/adminsimp/wp-content/uploads/sites/25/2025/02/Credentialing-Transition-to-CAQH_Health-Plan-Process_V7.pdf

## KEY FACT: As of January 1, 2024, CAQH is the mandated credentialing vendor for ALL Washington State health plans
- WA State law (RCW 48.43.750) requires all health carriers to use the state-designated electronic database (now CAQH)
- Maximum 90 days for credentialing determination; average must be ≤60 days

## Notes
- Coordinated Care/Ambetter: ~1 week for credentialing that doesn't need a new contract
- Kaiser WA: 60-day average; providers must follow instructions on website to initiate contracts or add practitioners to existing contracts
- Premera: As of 03/01/2025, CAQH only (no more paper/fax/email). CertifyOS is their CVO as of 05/01/2025
- Regence: 33 days TAT; requires CP 575 or 147C letter for Tax ID verification; EFT enrollment required
- Molina: Requires NPIs registered with WA Health Care Authority (HCA) FIRST before contracting
