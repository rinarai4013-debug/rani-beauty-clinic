# Premera Blue Cross Credentialing (Washington State)

## Key Requirements
- Must reside/practice primarily in Washington State
- Must be credentialed BEFORE requesting a contract
- Uses CAQH (Council for Affordable Quality Healthcare) for electronic credentialing applications

## Application Process
1. Meet credentialing criteria and standards (PDF available at premera.com)
2. Complete CAQH application at www.caqh.org (sign in to CAQH)
3. Complete provider credentialing checklist (PDF)
4. Submit credentialing request through the credentialing intake portal
5. Receive confirmation email
6. Premera works with "Certify" (NCQA-certified credentialing verification organization) for verification

## Facility Providers
- Send email to request contract
- Include: Legal name, DBA name, Facility tax ID number, Facility specialty, Facility address
- Premera will send a facility intake form for credentialing if needed

## Timeline
- Allow 30-45 days for response to credentialing application (per new provider onboarding page)
