## Bidding Strategy

**Phase 1 — Launch (Weeks 1–4):**
- Strategy: **Maximize Clicks** with manual CPC caps
- CPC caps: $3–$5 for aesthetic services, $5–$8 for medical weight loss, $2–$4 for wellness
- Match types: **Exact + Phrase only**
- No broad match until 60+ conversions and all gates fully passed
- Heavy negative keyword lists from Day 1

**Phase 2 — Optimization (Weeks 5–8, requires 30+ conversions):**
- Transition top-performing campaigns to **Maximize Conversions**
- Maintain CPC caps on lower-volume campaigns
- Begin testing phrase match expansion on proven winners

**Phase 3 — Scaling (Week 9+, requires 60+ conversions):**
- Target CPA bidding on campaigns with sufficient data
- Evaluate LegitScript certification for drug-name campaign unlock
- Consider remarketing for non-sensitive-category services
