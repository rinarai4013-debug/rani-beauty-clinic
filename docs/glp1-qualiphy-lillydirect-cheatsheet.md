# GLP-1 Operations Cheat Sheet — Qualiphy + LillyDirect

> **Internal use only — For Rina and staff. NOT for patients.**

---

## 1. Creating a Patient in Qualiphy

1. Log in to Qualiphy dashboard (qualiphy.com/provider)
2. Click **"New Patient"** or **"Submit GFE Request"**
3. Enter patient info:
   - Full legal name (must match ID)
   - Date of birth
   - Email address (patient will receive telehealth link here)
   - Phone number
4. Select exam type: **"Weight Management / GLP-1"**
5. Cost: **$27 per patient** (billed to Rani)

## 2. What to Upload for Each Patient

Upload the following to the Qualiphy patient file:

- [ ] **Completed intake form** (from /glp1/intake — print or export PDF)
- [ ] **Recent lab results** (within 6 months):
  - CBC (Complete Blood Count)
  - CMP (Comprehensive Metabolic Panel)
  - A1C (Hemoglobin A1C)
  - Lipid Panel
  - TSH (Thyroid)
- [ ] **Photo ID** (driver's license or passport)
- [ ] **Height, weight, BMI** (from intake form)
- [ ] **Medical history summary** (conditions, medications, allergies)

**If patient doesn't have recent labs:**
- Option A: Order through local lab (Quest/LabCorp) — patient pays directly
- Option B: Refer to PCP for updated labs
- Timeline: Labs typically take 1-3 business days

## 3. Submitting for GFE (Good Faith Exam)

1. In Qualiphy, open the patient record
2. Click **"Submit for GFE"**
3. Confirm all documents are uploaded
4. Select medication preference:
   - **Zepbound (tirzepatide)** — preferred for most patients
   - **Wegovy (semaglutide)** — alternative option
5. Add any clinical notes:
   - Patient's BMI
   - Relevant comorbidities (diabetes, PCOS, hypertension, sleep apnea)
   - Prior GLP-1 experience (if any)
   - Current dose if transitioning from another program
6. Submit — Qualiphy provider will schedule telehealth evaluation with patient

**Turnaround:** Typically 24-48 hours from submission to GFE completion.

## 4. After GFE Approval

When the Qualiphy provider approves the patient:

1. You'll receive notification in Qualiphy dashboard
2. Prescription is sent electronically — NOT to a pharmacy
3. Patient needs to fill through **LillyDirect** (for Zepbound) or preferred pharmacy (for Wegovy)
4. Send the **WELCOME_4_APPROVED** email/SMS sequence to patient
5. Update patient status in Airtable: Set GLP-1 status to "Approved"

**If GFE is denied:**
- Review denial reason in Qualiphy
- Common denials: BMI too low, contraindicated conditions, incomplete labs
- Contact patient to discuss next steps
- May need additional labs or documentation for resubmission

## 5. Walking Patient Through LillyDirect Setup

**Phone script / talking points:**

> "Great news — your prescription is approved! Let me walk you through getting your medication. It's super easy."

### Step-by-step for patient:

1. **Go to LillyDirect.com** on their phone or computer
2. **Create an account** with the same email used for Qualiphy
3. **Verify identity** — may need to confirm DOB and last 4 of SSN
4. **Prescription will appear** in their account (may take up to 24h after approval)
5. **Select self-pay pricing:**
   - Zepbound 2.5mg (starter): **$349/mo**
   - Zepbound 5mg: **$499/mo**
   - Zepbound 7.5mg–15mg: **$449/mo**
6. **Enter payment info** and shipping address
7. **Medication ships** to their door in 3-5 business days
8. Pen devices — pre-filled, ready to use, once weekly injection

**Important:** LillyDirect pricing is self-pay ONLY. If patient has insurance that covers Zepbound, they can use a regular pharmacy instead.

## 6. Savings Card Activation

### Zepbound Savings Card
1. Go to **zepbound.lilly.com/savings**
2. Click **"Activate Your Savings Card"**
3. Enter patient info as prompted
4. Eligibility: Must NOT have government insurance (Medicare, Medicaid, Tricare)
5. Savings amount varies by situation:
   - With commercial insurance: May reduce copay significantly
   - Without insurance: LillyDirect self-pay pricing already reflects best available rate
6. Save the card number — can be used at any pharmacy if filling outside LillyDirect

### Wegovy Savings Card
1. Go to **wegovy.com/savings-offer**
2. Follow prompts to activate
3. Can save up to certain amount per fill with eligible commercial insurance
4. Self-pay patients: introductory pricing of **$199/mo for first 2 months** on low dose

**Note:** Savings card programs change frequently. Check manufacturer websites for current offers before walking patient through activation.

### When to Request New GFE for Dose Change
- Each dose increase **may** require a new GFE depending on Qualiphy's protocol
- Submit new GFE request at least **1 week before** patient runs out of current dose
- Include: current dose, duration on dose, side effects, weight lost, reason for increase
- Cost: Another **$27** per GFE

### When NOT to Increase Dose
- Patient is losing weight steadily on current dose
- Significant side effects (nausea, GI issues) not yet resolved
- Patient preference to stay on current dose
- Less than 4 weeks on current dose

## 8. Troubleshooting Common Issues

### "My prescription isn't showing in LillyDirect"
- Check that patient used the same email as Qualiphy registration
- Allow up to 24-48 hours after GFE approval
- Contact LillyDirect support: 1-833-808-1234
- Worst case: Qualiphy can resend the prescription

### "I'm having bad nausea"
- Very common in first 2-4 weeks, especially after dose increases
- Tips to share: Eat smaller meals, avoid greasy/heavy foods, stay hydrated
- Take injection in the evening before bed
- If severe: May need to stay at current dose longer before titrating up
- If persistent vomiting: Contact prescribing provider — may need anti-nausea Rx

### "I stopped losing weight (plateau)"
- Normal — plateaus happen, especially at months 3-4
- Review: Nutrition adherence, exercise, sleep, stress
- May need dose titration if on lower dose
- Body composition check — may be losing fat but gaining lean mass
- Consider adding: Nutrition counseling, increased protein intake

### "I want to stop the medication"
- Schedule a check-in to discuss
- Gradual taper recommended (reduce dose before stopping)
- Discuss weight maintenance plan
- Continue nutrition and lifestyle support
- Cross-sell opportunity: Transition to maintenance medspa services

### "Insurance wants to cover it now"
- Great! Patient can switch to regular pharmacy with insurance
- They'll need prescribing provider to send Rx to their preferred pharmacy
- Contact Qualiphy to arrange pharmacy transfer
- Patient may still want to keep Rani program for support/check-ins

### "LillyDirect says my card was declined"
- Verify payment info is correct
- Some banks flag first-time medical purchases — patient should call their bank
- Try a different payment method
- Contact LillyDirect support for assistance
