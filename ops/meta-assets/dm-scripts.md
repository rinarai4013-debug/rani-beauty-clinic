## DM Rules

- Respond within business-hour SLA whenever possible
- Never diagnose in DMs
- Never prescribe in DMs
- Never discuss protected health details casually
- Move conversation toward correct next step
- Short, warm, human, clear
- No pressure language
- No fake urgency
