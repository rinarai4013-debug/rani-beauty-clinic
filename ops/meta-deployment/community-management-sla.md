# Community Management SLA

Source: `META-COMMANDER-OS-v8.md` — Community Management Rules

---

## Response SLAs

| Channel | Buying Intent | General | Spam |
|---------|--------------|---------|------|
| DMs | Same day | 1 business day | Ignore |
| Comments | Same day | 24 hours | Hide immediately |
| Reviews | 48 hours | 48 hours | Report if fake |
| Story Mentions | 24 hours | 24 hours | N/A |
| Tagged Posts | 24 hours | 24 hours | N/A |

---

## Buying Intent Signals (Prioritize These)

- asks about pricing
- asks about availability
- asks "how do I book?"
- asks about a specific service
- says "I want this" or "I need this"
- tags a friend with intent
- responds to an ad with a question
- fills out a form but doesn't book
- DMs after viewing a Story or Reel

---

## Lead Priority Order

1. Ready-to-book buyers
2. Pricing questions
3. Eligibility / process questions
4. Waitlist interest
5. General engagement

---

## Daily Checklist

- [ ] Check and respond to all DMs (buying intent first)
- [ ] Check and respond to all comments on recent posts and ads
- [ ] Check for new reviews on Google and Facebook
- [ ] Check Story mentions and tagged posts
- [ ] Check and respond to any lead form submissions
- [ ] Log unanswered count at end of day (target: zero)

---

## Escalation Rules

Escalate to owner or manager if:
- complaint mentions safety outcome, refund demand, or legal threat
- person claims they were ignored after submitting a form
- person requests medical advice that should not be handled in DM
- a creator / influencer / public figure wants a collaboration
- press or media inquiry

---

## Never Do In Public

- discuss medication details too specifically
- discuss patient records
- explain complex medical decisions
- admit fault before facts are known
- contradict clinic governance to "save the sale"

---

## Community Health Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Average DM Response Time | < 4 hours (buying intent) | Manual log or CRM |
| Average Comment Response Time | < 4 hours (buying intent) | Manual log |
| DM Backlog | 0 at end of day | Daily count |
| Comment Backlog | 0 at end of day | Daily count |
| Review Response Rate | 100% within 48 hours | Review platform |
| Unresolved Escalations | 0 | Escalation log |
| SLA Compliance Rate | > 95% | Weekly calculation |

---

## Patient-Generated Before/After Protocol

If a patient tags the clinic in a before/after post:

1. Acknowledge warmly (do not repost)
2. Check for written media consent
3. If no consent, request privately
4. Compliance review before any repost
5. If approved, repost with: "Shared with patient permission. Individual experiences and outcomes vary."
6. If not approved, do not repost

**Rule:** Patient-generated content is not automatically safe for clinic reposting just because the patient posted it first.
