### Need to Apply:

#### SkinCeuticals (L'Oreal)
- **Tab open:** skinceuticals.com
- **How to apply:** Go to skinceuticals.com → scroll to footer → look for "For Professionals" or "Professional Partners" link → OR call 1-800-811-1660 and ask for the professional accounts team
- **What you'll need:** Medical license, practice name, NPI number, DEA number (if applicable), practice address
- **Products to order once approved:** C E Ferulic ($185 retail), Triple Lipid Restore ($155), Retinol 0.3 ($80), Retinol 0.5 ($90), Glycolic 10 Renew ($96), Physical Fusion SPF 50 ($72)
- **Expected wholesale cost:** ~50% off retail = ~$1,420 for initial order of 22 units

#### iS Clinical
- **Tab open:** cpskinhealthpro.com/auth/registration
- **This is their professional portal.** Complete the registration form.
- **What you'll need:** Practice info, medical license, proof of medspa
- **Products to order once approved:** Cleansing Complex ($49 retail), Pro-Heal Serum Advance+ ($155)
- **Expected wholesale cost:** ~50% off = ~$334 for initial order of 7 units

#### Alastin (Galderma)
- **Tab open:** alastin.com
- **How to apply:** Scroll to footer → click "New Health Care Providers" → OR contact Galderma directly (your Allergan/Galderma rep if you have one for Botox/fillers)
- **What you'll need:** Practice credentials, Galderma account may help (if you already buy Restylane/Sculptra/Dysport)
- **Products to order once approved:** Regenerating Skin Nectar ($258), Ultra Nourishing Moisturizer ($98)
- **Expected wholesale cost:** ~50% off = ~$583 for initial order of 7 units

#### Biologique Recherche
- **How to apply:** Contact Rescue Spa (US distributor) at rescuespa.net or call them. You need to be an authorized esthetician/spa.
- **Products to order:** Lotion P50 ($155 retail)
- **Expected wholesale cost:** ~50% off = ~$234 for 3 units

#### DefenAge
- **How to apply:** Visit defenage.com → look for "For Professionals" → or contact their sales team directly
- **Products to order:** 8-in-1 BioSerum ($185 retail)
- **Expected wholesale cost:** ~50% off = ~$279 for 3 units

#### Sulwhasoo / Amorepacific / Innisfree
- **How to apply:** Contact Amorepacific US corporate for professional/spa wholesale accounts. These are selective distribution — you may need to go through Sephora's wholesale program or contact Amorepacific directly.
- **Alternative:** Buy at retail from Sephora for display stock while negotiating wholesale terms (markup will be lower but you get product on shelf immediately)
