## Event Types Reference
- **HARD-STOP:** Patient disqualified (MTC, MEN2, T1D, pregnancy, breastfeeding, eating disorder, BMI < 25)
- **SOFT-FLAG:** MD review required before proceeding
- **SAFETY-ALERT:** Active treatment safety concern
- **EMERGENCY:** Critical safety event (ER referral, 911)
- **MD-REVIEW:** Provider review completed
- **LAB-ALERT:** Lab results out of range
- **DOSE-CHANGE:** Titration or medication change approved
- **PREGNANCY:** Pregnancy reported (immediate discontinuation)
- **COMPLIANCE-HOLD:** Treatment held pending labs, GFE, or payment
