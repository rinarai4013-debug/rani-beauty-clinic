## The Traditional Clinic Problem

Most weight loss clinics and hormone therapy providers work with a single pharmacy or supplier. That means you get whatever that one pharmacy offers at whatever price they set. If their supply chain has a disruption, your treatment gets delayed. If their pricing increases, your costs go up. If they do not carry a specific formulation, you simply cannot access it.

This one-size-fits-all approach limits your options and often inflates your costs. It also means the clinic has less ability to advocate for you on price or quality because they have no alternative to offer.
