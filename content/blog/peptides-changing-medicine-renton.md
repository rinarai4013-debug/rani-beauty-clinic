## What Are Peptides, Exactly?

Peptides are short chains of amino acids, the same building blocks that make up proteins. Your body produces thousands of peptides naturally, and they act as signaling molecules, telling your cells, organs, and systems what to do. Think of them as highly specific instructions delivered directly to the parts of your body that need them most.

As you age, your body produces fewer of these signaling peptides. The result? Slower recovery, reduced growth hormone output, declining cellular energy, and the gradual erosion of the functions you once took for granted.

Peptide therapy reintroduces these signaling molecules, restoring communication pathways that have gone quiet. The treatments are prescribed by a licensed provider, medically supervised, and compounded by a licensed pharmacy.
