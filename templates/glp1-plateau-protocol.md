## Plateau: Lifestyle Reset (Email)

**Subject:** Let's break through this plateau together, [FIRST_NAME]

Hi [FIRST_NAME],

First, I want you to know that plateaus are NORMAL. Your body is incredibly smart and it adjusts to weight loss. This is not a sign that [MEDICATION] stopped working.

Here are some things that can help get things moving again:

**Hydration** — Are you drinking at least 64 oz of water daily? Dehydration can stall weight loss.

**Protein** — Aim for 60-80g of protein daily. This protects muscle and keeps your metabolism strong.

**Sleep** — Poor sleep directly impacts weight loss hormones. Aim for 7-8 hours.

**Movement** — Even 20 minutes of walking daily makes a difference. Your body responds to variety, so try something new!

**Stress** — Cortisol is a weight loss killer. If stress is high, even 5 minutes of deep breathing helps.

I'm also looking at whether a dose adjustment might help. Our Medical Director will review your progress.

You have NOT failed. This is a normal part of the journey. Let's work through it together!
