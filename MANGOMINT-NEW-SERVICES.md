## NEW TAGS TO ADD IN MANGOMINT

### Pharmacy Source Tags
- `QUALIPHYRX-GREENWICH` — Patient's medication sourced from QualiphyRx Greenwich
- `OLYMPIA-PHARMACY` — Patient's medication sourced from Olympia Pharmacy
- `HALLANDALE-ORDER` — Patient's medication sourced from Hallandale

### Service Tags (New)
- `TRT-PATIENT` — Men's testosterone replacement
- `HRT-FEMALE` — Women's hormone replacement
- `SKINCARE-PATIENT` — Rx skincare (Tighten, NADvantage, retinoids)
- `BELLA-PATIENT` — Bella #1 SR subscriber
- `SEXUAL-HEALTH` — Any sexual health service
- `ED-PATIENT` — Men's erectile dysfunction program
- `TRIMIX-PATIENT` — Men's TriMix injectable ED
- `INTIMATE-WELLNESS` — Women's intimate wellness products

### Status Tags (New)
- `FREE-KIT-SENT` — Free injection kit shipped to patient
- `CROSS-SELL-ACTIVE` — Currently receiving a cross-sold product

### Revenue Tags (New)
- `BUDGET-OPTION` — Patient on troche/oral alternative (price-sensitive)
