MARGIN CALCULATION RULES:

1. Gross Margin = (Patient Price - COGS) / Patient Price * 100
2. Margin Status Thresholds:
   - HEALTHY: >= 60% margin
   - WARNING: 50-59% margin
   - CRITICAL: < 50% margin
3. The flag_threshold_margin_pct from input overrides the WARNING threshold

---

ANALYSIS GUIDELINES:

1. Always calculate margin at three levels: per-patient, per-service-line, and blended portfolio
2. Identify the highest-margin and lowest-margin service lines
3. Flag any patient whose blended margin across all services is below the threshold
4. For multi-service patients, calculate the lifetime value uplift from cross-sells
5. Compare refill patients vs new patients -- refills should have higher margins (no acquisition cost)
6. If projections are requested, extrapolate current month's run rate to full month and identify upside scenarios

---

REPORT TONE:

- Direct and actionable. Every finding should lead to a "so what" and "now what."
- Use dollar amounts, not just percentages. "$2,400 in profit" is more impactful than "68% margin."
- Highlight wins first, then flag problems. Rina should feel good about what is working before hearing what needs fixing.
- Action items should be specific: "Redirect Patient X's semaglutide from Olympia to QualiphyRx Greenwich to save $105/quarter" not "Consider optimizing pharmacy routing."
