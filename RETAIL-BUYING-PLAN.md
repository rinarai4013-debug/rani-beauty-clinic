### Why 62.4% and Not 50%

K-Beauty wholesale is cheaper than Western medical-grade. Your K-Beauty products cost 30-40% of retail (vs. 50% for SkinCeuticals/EltaMD). This pulls your blended margin UP. The K-Beauty products also have higher velocity (lower price = less purchase resistance = more units sold), so they drive volume while the clinical products drive dollar margin.

### Revenue Per Category (What Drives the Business)

| Revenue Driver | How It Works |
|----------------|-------------|
| **Volume engine:** K-Beauty serums + SPF | $17-25 price points. Low resistance. High repurchase. Clients buy 2-3 per visit. |
| **Margin engine:** SkinCeuticals + Alastin + DefenAge | $90-258 price points. $45-129 profit per unit. Provider-prescribed. |
| **Repeat engine:** SPF + Snail Mucin + Lip Mask | Daily use = runs out fast = repurchase every 4-8 weeks. |
